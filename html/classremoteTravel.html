Detailed Description

The class defines a remote place to stay, such as a hotel, and a vehicle in which to travel there (e.g. a plane)

At the moment the assumption is that this is effectively a holiday, where agents will stay at a single hotel
with a single way to get there - this means that at present one can't travel to a hotel other than by a single fixed travel vehicle
however, agents could accumulate at a single hotel by travelling there at different times.
The hotel currently has infinte capacity. Hilbert would be pleased. A capacity test could be included by adding a counter in the visit method
